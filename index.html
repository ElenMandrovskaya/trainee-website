Dogni - exclusive handmade bowls for animals

Our little story

The basis for the manufacture of bowls is high quality Ukrainian ceramics, the same as in the dishes. After drying, the bowl is covered with a glassy glaze and annealed at t > 1000 ° C. We do not use lead-based glazes, our glazes are based on kaolin (clay) and quartz, which ensures absolute safety for the animal. The colors are not applied with a brush, but mixed with the glaze. This technology allows you not to worry about color fastness, it does not fade or wash off. The bowl is thick-walled and heavy in the lower part, which provides stability during use. And yes it is handmade.
